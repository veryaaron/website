What kind of event is Elsewhere?

Elsewhere is a temporary participatory community event inspired by the principles of the global Burning Man cultural movement. The Event is created collectively by you and every participant through art, collaboration, and shared responsibility.

Elsewhere is not a traditional commercial festival. You contribute to the experience through your presence, participation, and respect for the community guidelines established by NCA.

The Event takes place in a remote and challenging environment and requires a high degree of personal responsibility and self-reliance.
