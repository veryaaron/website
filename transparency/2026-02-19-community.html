Overview

Third community meeting of the Nobodies Collective Asociación (NCA). The meeting covered production progress on vendor management and financial planning, permit and compliance preparations, MetaLeads meeting updates, the Humans volunteer platform nearing launch, a legal update on asset procurement, the Governance Working Group’s go/no-go decision process and timeline, collaborator onboarding progress, and a community vote on alternative event names.

  • The community may already be in the “80 to 800” phase given the number of active participants
  • Meeting held on Google Meet instead of Zoom; noted that chat messages would not be saved

Key Community Questions & Discussion

Containers

Multiple questions about the timeline for securing container access. Pepe confirmed this is the number one priority being actively accelerated. A clearer timeline is expected in a couple of weeks. The community was reminded that containers hold critical build infrastructure, though some teams have demonstrated they can build without knowing container contents.

Community Outreach

A community member asked about reaching out to the broader community. Pepe explained that GDPR compliance requires careful handling of mailing lists. Personal outreach (telling friends about the association) is not a GDPR issue and is encouraged.

Ticket Sales Timeline

Anna asked about the timeline for ticket sales and the event website. Daniel confirmed tickets will not go on sale until the go/no-go process is complete, providing at least until February 28–29. Participant wellness content (code of conduct, inclusion policies) needs to be on the website before sales begin. A separate event website will be needed once the event name is confirmed.

FIST Volunteer System

Ben F asked about using the open-source FIST software for volunteer task management. Peter confirmed the system is ready to deploy and could be launched quickly once a domain is available. There are some known issues to fix, potentially with AI assistance.
