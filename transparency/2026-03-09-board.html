NCA Board Meeting Minutes

Date: Sunday 9 March 2026, 18:58 CET
Board members present: Daniel T, Peter D, Michelle T, Ben D, Chris O, Ward D, Pablo V, Victor P
Non-board attendees: Pepe C (Legal Counsel), Daniela M S (Production Manager)
Facilitator: Chris O

1. Statute Amendment Vote

The board considered voting on proposed statute amendments prepared by Pepe C (including provisions for asynchronous and electronic voting). The vote was deferred as Peter D and Michelle T had not yet reviewed the proposed changes. The amendments are required in order to formally and legally accept the first batch of association collaborators.

Decision: Vote deferred. Board members to review before next meeting.

2. Bank Account Update

The association is officially registered with the Spanish government and holds a temporary NIF number. All required documentation has been submitted to Sabadell bank. Daniel T committed to visiting the bank daily until the account is opened.

3. Ticketing Update

Ticket sales are targeted for Thursday/Friday of this week. Prerequisites discussed:

  • FIST system deployment (not a blocker for initial sales, per Peter D)
  • Event name (resolved, see below)
  • Terms & Conditions and refund policy (due Wednesday from Pepe C)
  • Code of conduct
  • Low-income ticket pricing (expected from Ripham (Fran))

Daniela M S will project manage the ticket release process. Pablo V will investigate early entry ticketing options.

4. Event Name

The board considered three candidate names: Elsewhere, Nobody’s, and Nobody’s Here. Following community polling and board discussion:

Decision: The event will be named “Elsewhere”, subject to trademark clearance.

  • Vote: majority in favour; Pablo V did not vote as the majority was already reached
  • Ward D requested their disagreement be formally noted, citing concern that the decision was based on emotional considerations rather than legal risk
  • Pepe C will commence trademark clearance

5. Event Site Location

The board discussed whether to hold the event at the current site or move to a new location.

Decision: The event will be held at the current (old) site.

  • Vote: majority in favour; Pablo V abstained; Ward D voted against and requested their disagreement be formally noted
  • Parallel planning for a new site for a 2027 event is ongoing; preparation would need to begin around June 2026

6. Production Transition

Daniela M S announced the transition from assessment to production mindset. A new process was agreed:

  • Daniela will present 5 weekly board action items to delegate/distribute
  • Goal: board to hand off 10 items per week, reducing bottleneck

7. Community Engagement

The board discussed an outreach strategy for community members with concerns about recent decisions:

  • Daniel T proposed an inclusive call on all channels
  • Michelle T proposed a risk register spreadsheet for community input
  • Decision: hold off on posting the call until the spreadsheet is set up first

8. Legal Update — Container Assets

A meeting is scheduled for Wednesday 2pm to discuss options for sourcing containers for the event. Legal counsel is reviewing arrangements to ensure compliance. Further details are restricted.

9. Board Documentation Policy

The board agreed to a three-tier classification system for documents:

  • Confidential — board only, not shared
  • Restricted — limited distribution
  • Public — majority of content

Daniel T will implement a board confidential storage approach, with AI-assisted sanitisation for publicly shared minutes.

10. Daniela M S Contractor Agreement

Decision: Approved. Daniela M S’s one-month contractor agreement for continued event organisation was approved unanimously. Rate confirmed as fair and consistent with previous work.
