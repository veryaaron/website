What is this?

This year we’re offering travel expense reimbursements for volunteers who commit to early set-up or full strike. It’s not a ticket discount — it’s a reimbursement of your actual travel costs, processed after the event.

Places are limited and we’ll prioritise volunteers for our most critical roles — infrastructure build and cantina — so the sooner you apply, the better.

Everyone still buys a ticket. This covers your travel costs, nothing else. You show up, you do the work, you submit receipts, we pay you back.

How it works

  1. Get your ticket for Elsewhere 2026 — buy one here
  2. Sign up on Humans at humans.nobodies.team and add yourself to the relevant set-up or strike shifts
  3. Fill in the application form below
  4. If approved, submit your travel receipts after the event to travelclaims@nobodies.team
  5. Reimbursement is processed in July/August
